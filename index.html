About Us

Preserving Tradition Through Culinary Excellence

At Vignesh Catering and Event Management, we honor the rich culinary heritage of South India with every meal we prepare. For over three generations, our family has preserved authentic Brahmin recipes and traditional cooking methods.

Our journey began in the temple towns of Tamil Nadu and Kerala, where we mastered the art of Sattvic cooking - pure vegetarian cuisine prepared with devotion and mindfulness.

Today, we bring these sacred flavors and cultural elegance to your most important celebrations, creating memorable experiences deeply rooted in South Indian tradition.

Traditional Virundhu Menu

Traditional Serving Order

Our virundhu (feast) is served on fresh banana leaves following traditional South Indian customs, with items placed in specific positions according to ritual significance.

1.

Salt, pickle, and payasam are served first (top left)

2.

Banana and papadams (right side)

3.

Rice with ghee followed by sambar rice

4.

Rasam rice and curd rice to complete the meal
